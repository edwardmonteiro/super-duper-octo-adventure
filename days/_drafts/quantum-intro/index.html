What is a 'quantum'?

Energy comes in discrete packets, not a smooth ramp.

Continuous (classical) Discrete (quantum)

1900. Max Planck noticed that energy seems to come in tiny indivisible packets. He called the packet a quantum.

Think of climbing a staircase instead of a ramp: you can stand on step 1 or step 2, but never at "step 1.4".

A photon is a quantum of light. A qubit, later, will be a quantum of information.
